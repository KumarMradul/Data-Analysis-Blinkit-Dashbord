📊 Dashboard Summary: Blinkit Sales Analysis

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of Blinkit's sales performance, customer behavior, and outlet trends.

🔑 Key Metrics
💰 Total Sales: ~1.20M
📦 Total Items Sold: ~8.5K
⭐ Average Rating: ~3.9
📊 Average Sales per Item: ~140


🛍️ Sales Insights


      Low Fat products contribute significantly higher sales compared to regular items
      Certain product categories (like grocery essentials) dominate overall revenue
      A few top-performing items drive a major portion of total sales



🏪 Outlet Insights


      Sales vary based on outlet establishment year
      Some outlet types and sizes perform better in terms of revenue generation
      Older outlets tend to show stable and consistent sales trends
📈 Performance Analysis


      Clear variation in sales across different product categories
      Customer ratings indicate moderate satisfaction levels
      Sales distribution highlights opportunities for product and outlet optimization
🔍 Key Takeaways


      Focus on high-performing product categories can boost revenue
      Improving low-rated products can enhance customer satisfaction
      Expansion strategy should consider top-performing outlet types and locations
